back

The world is being shaken by the constant demonstrations happening in different countries all over the world. They all have something in common, people are being united to fight for something bigger than them, for their own rights, and they want to be heard. You can be part of this fight by joining them in a peaceful virtual way.


With this website, you are able to support them at a distance, you don’t have to be near in a physical way but with your heart. But how can you demonstrate your support?

The aim is doing as much noise as we can to show our loudness.

01

Explore what is happening around the world and get in touch with the different protest. Then, select the country you want to support and get deeper into your understanding of the topic.

02

When you feel ready you can join the peaceful fight and make some noise.

03

Fill the form with your data and watch what happens!
